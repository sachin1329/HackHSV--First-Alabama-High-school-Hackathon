ABOUT HACKHSV

HackHSV is an MLH high school hackathon in the Rocket City (Huntsville, AL)! HackHSV is dedicated to solving real world issues, and participating in it is a great way to meet new people, learn new skills, and, of course, code.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHEN IS IT?

The competition is expected to be on April 1, 2017. You can find the full schedule below.

WHERE IS IT?

HackHSV is taking place at the Shield Jones Athletic Complex at Randolph School's Garth Campus - 4915 Garth Rd SE, Huntsville, Alabama

WHO CAN ATTEND HACKHSV?

Anyone ages 15-18 from any part of the world can register. If you love computer science and want to have a fun time, then you are welcome at HackHSV.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

All you need to bring is a waiver with a parent's signature and a photo ID. If you do not bring these, you will not be allowed to enter the competition. A computer with a charges are also good ideas to have.

WHAT CAN I WIN?

We have plenty of awesome prizes that will be provided by our incredible sponsors and MLH. Prizes will be given out to winners of first, second, and third place as well as to superlative categories.

HARDWARE?

We have plenty of hardware for you to work with - Arduino, breadboards, and Oculus Rifts to name a few.

WHAT ARE THE RULES?

All the rules that you have to follow are under MLH's Code of Conduct. All rules are strickly enforced, and any violations will not be tolerated.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

HackHSV does not cost anything. Registering along with all food, T-Shirts, and prizes are free! The only thing you have to pay for is your travelling plans.

HAVE MORE QUESTIONS?

If you have anymore questions, drop us a line at hackhsv@gmail.com
